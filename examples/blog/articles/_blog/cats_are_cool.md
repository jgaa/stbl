---
title: Cats Are Cool
published: 2025-01-09 08:30
tags: pets, personal, home, cats
---

Cats are cool because they can switch from quiet observer to full sprint in two seconds. One minute my cat is watching rain at the window, and the next minute she is racing after a toy that she ignored all day.

I like how cats make a home feel lived in. They pick a favorite chair, claim a sun spot, and somehow make every cardboard box look valuable. Their routines are simple, but they pull people into those routines too.

The best part is personality. Some cats are social and noisy, others are calm and private, and most are a mix depending on the hour. Learning those patterns is half the fun of sharing space with them.
